Featured Cigar of the Month

Introduced in 2018, the Crux Epicure Maduro Short Salomone is a 6 x 54 salomone. Its Mexican San Andres wrapper over Nicaraguan binder and filler is hand rolled by Tabacalera AJ Fernandez Cigars de Nicaragua S.A. in Esteli, Nicaragua. It is a full-flavored, medium bodied cigar with hints of cocoa, molasses and cedar.

Featured Accessory of the Month

The Arturo Fuente ceramic ashtrays symbolize their high standards and consistent quality that cigar smokers have come to expect from this iconic brand. The vibrant ashtrays measure 8.5 inches and 2.75 inches high and reads "We will never rush the hands of time" around the rim.
